# Pokemon Locations

A human-readable guide to where every wild Pokemon and in-game trade can be found in Poketrainer Levels. Locations are organized by game progression.

**Legend:**
- ★ = Species new to this version (not in vanilla FireRed)
- 🌙 = Night-only (appears only during odd play-time hours)
- Species without a moon icon are available during the day, and usually at night too unless noted

> **Day/Night cycle:** Night occurs during odd play-time hours (hour 1, 3, 5, …). Day occurs during even hours (hour 0, 2, 4, …). The cycle repeats every 2 hours of play time. At night, some species are added, and some day-only species are swapped out. Night also has a slight visual cue: outdoor palettes are a bit darker and bluer.

## Species Only Available Via Trade

The following species can **only** be obtained through in-game NPC trades:

- **Johto starters:** Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Totodile
- **Hoenn starters:** Treecko, Torchic, Mudkip
- **Special:** Castform, Kecleon
- **Johto legends:** Raikou, Entei, Suicune, Ho-Oh
- **Mewtwo trades (pick one):** Mew, Celebi, Regirock, Regice, Registeel, Kyogre, Groudon, Rayquaza, Latias, Latios, Jirachi
